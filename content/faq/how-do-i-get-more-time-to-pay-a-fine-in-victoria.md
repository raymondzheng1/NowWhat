If a Victorian fine is more than you can pay right now, you have options. Acting early —
before the fine becomes a "Notice of Final Demand" — keeps more of them open.

## Ask for a payment plan

You can ask Fines Victoria to pay the fine off in smaller amounts over time, instead of
all at once. This is free to set up.

## A Work and Development Permit

If you're in hardship, you may be able to clear the fine through a Work and Development
Permit. This lets you "work off" the fine through things like treatment, counselling, a
course, or volunteering, with the help of a support worker or organisation.

## Other options

If you disagree with the fine itself, you can ask for a review on set grounds, or ask to
have it heard in court. What's available depends on the stage your fine has reached.

## Get free help

[Work out your options](/start) in a few steps. Victoria Legal Aid gives free help with
fines, and the National Debt Helpline offers free financial counselling.
