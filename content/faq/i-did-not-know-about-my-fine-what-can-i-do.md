## There is a review option made for this situation

In Victoria, one ground for a review is that **you did not know about the fine**. This is called the 'person was unaware' ground.

You need to **act within 14 days of the day you found out** about the fine. Acting quickly matters. Your options can change as the fine moves through its stages.

## What you will likely need

To apply on this ground, you may need a **statutory declaration**. This is a written, signed statement. It explains when and how you found out about the fine.

Having the fine or infringement notice (including the infringement number) ready will also help.

## How to start

You can apply for a review with the agency that issued the fine, or with **Fines Victoria**.
