Renting · Notice to vacate

You can ask VCAT to review your notice to vacate.

What this is about

A notice to vacate is your rental provider asking you to leave. It must give a valid reason and the correct notice period. If you think it’s wrong, you can apply to VCAT (the tribunal) to challenge it — and there’s a deadline to do that.

This is general information to help you understand your options — not legal advice. For advice about your situation, see the free help below.
