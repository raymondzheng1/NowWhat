This is a general guide for a decision made by a Commonwealth (federal) government
agency when there isn't a more specific guide for your exact situation. Many
government decisions can be reviewed, but not all of them, and the time limits and
steps depend on the law the decision was made under. Because the details matter, the
safest next step is to talk to a free legal service, who can confirm whether your
decision can be reviewed and by when.

> This is the fallback entry. It deliberately stays general and routes the person to
> human help rather than asserting a pathway or deadline that may not apply to them.
