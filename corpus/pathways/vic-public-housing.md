Social-housing problems split into two separate paths, and getting into the right one
matters. Decisions about a housing *application* — eligibility, priority, a transfer, or
a rebate — are reviewed by the **Housing Appeals Office** and have no strict deadline.
An *eviction* (a notice to vacate) is a tenancy matter under the Residential Tenancies
Act, goes to **VCAT**, and has a 30-day window to challenge it early. The 30-day window
is the time to challenge *early* — a renter can still argue the notice is invalid at the
possession hearing.
