The NDIA has made a decision about whether you can access the NDIS, or about what is
in your plan. You can ask the NDIA to review the decision, and if you still disagree
you can ask an independent tribunal to review it for free. Free advocates can help you
through the process. There is a time limit, so it helps to find out the date early.

> All figures and time limits here are marked **VERIFY** and must be confirmed by a
> human before launch. The app does not state a specific deadline until that happens.
