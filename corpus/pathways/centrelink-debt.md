Centrelink (Services Australia) has made a decision about your payment, or says you
have a debt. In most cases you can ask them to look at the decision again, and if you
still disagree you can ask an independent tribunal to review it — and that review is
free. There is a time limit, so it helps to find out the date early.

> All of the figures and time limits in this entry are marked **VERIFY** and must be
> confirmed against the cited source by a human before launch. Until then the app does
> not state a specific deadline — it explains that a time limit applies and routes the
> person to a free service that can confirm it.
